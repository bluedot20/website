Capstone Program

Our Program assimilates university-level Capstone Programs to offer exceptional opportunities to high school students in multiple fields of interest, enabling them to deliver practical solutions for clients including renowned companies and organizations, university labs and fast developing companies, organizations, university labs, and fast devleoping startups.

We aim to provide students with a wide perspective of applicable opportunities to network and work for clients in fields that include, but not limited to Data Science, App Development, Robotics, Business Tech. and Design. Students learn to cooperate in teams, tackling real-world issues while interacting with industry experts.

Project Eco8

Client: Jeju Island’s Council of Sustainable Development

Duration: 2022-2023 Winter

  • Application Closed
  • In Progess

Supported by the Jeju island's Council of Sustainable Development, this project aims to harmonize between residents (including foreign visitors) and the breathtaking nature of Jeju's Island by digitizing a widely used local map that promotes sustainable development practices.

By mainly focusing on the 3R principles (reduce, reuse and recycle), work with over 100 independent service providers all over Jeju Island to create a community engaging platform.

Completed Projects

The NFT Project

Client: Nabiya Cat Shelter (NGO)

Output: The NFT Project

Duration: 2022 Summer

  • Application Closed
  • Completed

This project is for students who are interested in design management and coding fields such as block-chain and web development. During this project, learn knowledge about the blockchain ecosystem and the NFT market issuing, promoting and selling NFT art assets.

Currently 5000 unique NFTs were designed and successfully minted to be ready for purchase on the market. All proceeds from this project are being donated to support the Nabiya Cat Shelter.

Project Planet Plus

Client: Korean Red Cross, The Promise

Output: The Project Planet Plus Deliverables

Duration: 2022 Spring

  • Application Closed
  • Completed

We are collaborating with the Korean Red Cross and The Promise, with a goal to digitize a board game designed by them. The final completed game aims to provide education on disaster response and will be made accessible by children globally.

The game was presented by the Red Cross at the APMCDRR 2022, convened by the UN, and is projected to be implemented in multiple APAC countries by 2023.
