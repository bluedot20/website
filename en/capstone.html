Project Eco8

Client: Jeju Island’s Council of Sustainable Development

Duration: 2023 Spring

  • Application Closed
  • In Progess

Supported by the Jeju island's Council of Sustainable Development, this project aims to harmonize between residents (including foreign visitors) and the breathtaking nature of Jeju's Island by digitizing a widely used local map that promotes sustainable development practices.

By mainly focusing on the 3R principles (reduce, reuse and recycle), work with over 100 independent service providers all over Jeju Island to create a community engaging platform.

Project CUBS

Client: myKstar (In collaboration with Columbia University 292 Consulting Team)

Duration: 2023 Spring

  • Application Closed
  • In Progess

This project aims to collaborate with MyKstar, a rising start-up, based in North America and South Korea to provide an innovative language-learning platform.

The students are divided into teams to deliver the following projects:

  • Analyzing and developing managerial and customer-based information by database implementation and data visualization (for admins) processing large handwritten datasets for finetuning image recognition models to be integrated into the platform.
  • Performing competitive analysis with NLP (Natural Learning Process) by analyzing customer feedback and comments for other competing platforms.
